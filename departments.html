Departments

To drive the development and growth of Nasarawa State's business environment and help businesses achieve their full potential, the Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Investment implements and oversees programs and networks that offer vital resources such as information, education, training, financing, and other forms of support. These initiatives are designed to promote sustainable economic development across the State. In pursuit of our mission and vision, the Ministry is supported by six dedicated Departments, each working diligently to bring this vision to fruition.

  2. Departments

Administration

The Administration Department oversees the daily operations of the Ministry, including the management of staff, public relations, legal affairs, and human resources (HR) needs. This department is responsible for monitoring the Ministry's performance and making strategic decisions to guide future initiatives and ensure organizational success.

Planning, Research & Statistics

The Directorate of Planning, Research, and Statistics, led by a Director, serves as the central hub of the organization. It coordinates the activities of other departments by formulating and implementing policies and programs designed to achieve the organization's mandate. The Directorate focuses on the overall development of standardization and quality assurance in Nasarawa State through a systematic, integrated, and coordinated approach.
