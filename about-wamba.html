About MTII

The Ministry of Trade, Industry & Investment is dedicated to fostering economic growth and creating opportunities for all communities within Nasarawa State. With its six specialized bureaus (departments), the Ministry works to enhance the state’s economic competitiveness, strengthen the domestic industry, and promote the creation of quality jobs across all regions of the state. Serving as the primary advocate for business within the state and at the federal level, the Ministry plays a vital role in supporting the needs of Nasarawa's citizens on a daily basis. Additionally, the Ministry drives innovation and fosters the development of key sectors, including agriculture, solid minerals, and tourism, which are central to Nasarawa State’s unique competitive advantages.

Our Mission

The Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Investment (MTII) is dedicated to fostering a dynamic and supportive ecosystem that drives sustainable economic growth and creates opportunities for all stakeholders, including investors, entrepreneurs, start-ups, and businesses across Nasarawa State. Our mission is to develop and maintain an enabling environment where innovation, enterprise, and investment can thrive.

By implementing policies that reduce barriers to business, promoting trade and industrial development, and providing strategic support to emerging and established businesses, we aim to empower our clients to succeed and contribute to the broader economic development of the state. Through collaboration, transparency, and continuous improvement, MTII is committed to ensuring that every individual and organization has the resources, guidance, and opportunities needed to prosper and make a meaningful impact.

Our Vision

The Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Investment (MTII) envisions a thriving and resilient economy driven by both local and international trade. We are committed to creating a robust and sustainable economic environment that fosters growth, innovation, and prosperity for businesses and individuals alike. By facilitating seamless trade relations, both within and beyond the borders of Nasarawa State, we aim to connect local industries with global markets, enabling them to compete, innovate, and succeed on an international scale.

Our vision is to position Nasarawa State as a hub for trade, industry, and investment, where opportunities for economic advancement are accessible to all, driving prosperity, job creation, and long-term development. Through strategic partnerships, policy advocacy, and resource empowerment, MTII strives to be the catalyst for transformative growth and a prosperous future for all.

Our Core Values

  • High standards of personal integrity and professional excellence: We uphold the highest ethical standards, ensuring that all actions are guided by integrity and a commitment to excellence in everything we do.
  • Mutual respect and teamwork: We foster a culture of respect and collaboration, valuing the contributions of all individuals and working together to achieve common goals.
  • Creativity and individual initiative: We encourage innovation and the proactive pursuit of new ideas, empowering individuals to take initiative and contribute to continuous improvement.
  • Transparency: We are committed to openness and honesty, ensuring that our processes, decisions, and actions are clear and accessible to all stakeholders.
Our Goals

Our Strategic Goals

Business innovation, equity, and resilience are the core principles that guide the efforts of the Nasarawa State Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Investment. These principles are fundamental to our primary objective of enhancing Nasarawa State’s competitiveness in the global economy. The Ministry is committed to a singular, overarching goal: to improve Nasarawa’s economic competitiveness both locally and globally. Each of the Ministry's strategic goals is designed to advance this mission and support the achievement of this critical objective.

Our Strategic Objectives

  1. Improve and Drive Nasarawa State's Agriculture, Innovation and Global Competitiveness
  2. Promotes Inclusive Equitable Economic Growth
  3. Help Local Farmers address the Climate Crisis Through Mitigation, Adaptation, and Resilience Efforts
  4. Use Data to Expand Opportunity and Discovery within the State
  5. Provide 21st Century Service with 21st Century Capabilities and Innovations
