Wamba LGC &
Engr. Abdullahi A. Sule's
Reforms

Wamba Local Government Council in Nasarawa State operates under the direct supervision of the Executive Governor, His Excellency Governor Abdullahi A. Sule. His dynamic leadership has been instrumental in enabling the Local Government to carry out its mission and objectives effectively. Governor Sule’s forward-thinking vision for economic growth and development has created an environment where unity, diversity, equity, trade, industry, and investment are prioritized for the state’s prosperity.

Under the leadership of Governor A.A Sule, Wamba LGC has been able to implement policies that align with the Governor’s broader goals for industrialization and economic diversification. Governor Sule’s commitment to creating a unified community and business-friendly environment, fostering public-private partnerships, and attracting both local and foreign investment has enabled the Local Government to facilitate significant progress in the development of Wamba LGC's communities and infrastructure.

Governor Sule's leadership style, which emphasizes innovation, accountability, and inclusivity, has allowed the Local Government to mobilize resources, streamline processes, and execute projects efficiently. Through Governor Sule's guidance, the Local Government has been able to promote unity, peace, equity, and enhance trade relations, promote small and medium enterprises, and ensure sustainable economic growth.

Wamba LGC in Nasarawa State is led by Honourable Ezekiel Musa Jagga, who serves as the Executive Chairman, providing strategic direction and overseeing the implementation of policies and programs. Honourable Ezekiel Musa Jagga is responsible for ensuring that the Local Government's objectives align with the overall vision of the state government, particularly in promoting unity, diversity, peace, equity, trade, industrial development, and attracting investment to the state. Under the guidance of the Executive Chairman, the Local Government plays a critical role in formulating and executing initiatives aimed at fostering economic growth and job creation in Wamba LGC.

Supporting the Executive Chairman is the Secretary of the Local Government, a career civil servant who acts as the administrative head of the Ministry. The Permanent Secretary is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the ministry, ensuring that activities are carried out efficiently and in compliance with government regulations. They manage the ministry’s workforce, oversee the execution of projects, and ensure that resources are properly allocated to meet the ministry’s goals. Together, the Honourable Commissioner and the Permanent Secretary work in close collaboration, ensuring that the Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Investment effectively delivers on its mandate to enhance trade, industrialization, and investment in the state.
