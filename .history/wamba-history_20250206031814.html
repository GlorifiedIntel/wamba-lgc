Our History

The Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Investment (MTII) was established following the creation of Nasarawa State on October 1, 1996, by the then Military Head of State, General Sani Abacha. Since its inception, the Ministry has been at the forefront of driving economic growth and development within the state.
